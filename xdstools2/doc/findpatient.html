Find Patient Help

This tool initiates the Find Patients XCPD to a Responding Gateway of your choice.

After running the request, use the Inspect Results button to browse the query response.

The sites listed as potential targets for this transaction depend on the sites configured in the tool. Responding Gateways are listed that have an endpoint for the XCPD transaction.

Note that when you change the TLS setting that the selectable sites change depending on which sites offer TLS/non-TLS endpoints.

Selecting a site under the category of responding Gateways is adequate. The toolkit will generate a XCPD transaction.
